Visit Chomutov!

The medieval city with a touch of socialism.

How to spent a beatiful day in Chomutov?

Spent a day by the Alum lake!

The Alum lake is a beatiful destination for swimming, sunbathing or camping.

Visit a city park!

The city park is great for having a picnic with your family or for a date with your dear counterpart.

See the Bezruč valley!

The Bezruč valley has a five-kilometres long bicycle path for skating or jogging and exposes a beatiful nature!
